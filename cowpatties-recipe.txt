Ingredients:
 - 2 cups sugar
 - 4 tablespoons cocoa
 - 1/2 cup of butter - 1 stick
 - 1/2 cup milk
 - 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
 - 1/2 cup peanut butter
 - 2 1/2 cups of oatmeal

Directions:
  1. Bring sugar, cocoa, butter, and milk to a rolling boil in a medium saucepan for exactly 1 1/2 	   minutes.
  2. Remove from heat and add vanilla, peanut butter, and oatmeal, stir ingredients well.
  3. Quickly spread on either wax paper or greased cookie sheet.
  4. Allow to cool and then cut into squares.
